Healthier Burger Helper

Description

The popcorn recipe is the simplest thing I could think of to finish the HTML portion of this assignment and move on to working with CSS. I'm snarky sometimes.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of microwave popcorn

Steps

  1. Remove plastic from popcorn bag
  2. Place popcorn bag in microwave, "this side facing up" facing, well, up
  3. Cook in microwave for approximately 2.5 minutes, or until popcorn pops begin to slow
  4. Try not to immediately burn your mouth on hot kernels.

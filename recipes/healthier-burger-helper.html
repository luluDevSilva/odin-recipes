Healthier Burger Helper

Description

"Healthier Burger Helper" is an iteration of the commercial Hamburger Helper boxed meal, for my picky husband, but safe for me to eat (as someone with food allergies).

Ingredients

  • 1 box banza pasta (I like elbows or cappavati shapes)
  • 1lb organic grass-fed ground beef
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1.5 cups Pacific Foods red pepper & tomato soup
  • 2T italian seasoning
  • 1T chili powder
  • 1T garlic powder
  • salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet with chili powder, salt, pepper, and half the other seasonings
  2. In a large pot, while beef is browning, boil pasta until al dente
  3. Drain pasta when al dente
  4. Combine drained pasta, Pacific Foods soup, ground beef, remaining seasonings and cheese
  5. Warm/combine over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce is thickened
  6. Serve with vegetable of choice: steamed broccoli, peas, etc.
